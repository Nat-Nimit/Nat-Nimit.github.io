WELCOME

I am a linguaphile who loves exploring the world of language and literature...
This webpage is created as a hub where my ideas are shared through past works and recent writings.
There are three sections that you might be interested in: Academic, Prose, and Poetry.

In Academic, all papers and works related to my fields of study are put together and represented in a more approachable style.

In Prose, memoirs, informal writings, and opinions are distributed in the form of blog.

In Poetry, it is here where I let my mind free in the form of rhythmic utterances crafted with meanings and contexts.
